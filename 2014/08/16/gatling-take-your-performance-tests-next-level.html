Gatling: Take your Performance Tests to the next level!

16 Aug 2014

A few months ago I've published an article for ThoughtWorks about Performance Tests with Gatling. Gatling is an open-source load testing tool written in Scala. One of the main points of this tool is writing performance tests through a DSL, easy to read, maintain and also treat it as production code.

The idea of this article was sharing my recent experience with a client where I had to develop some automated performance tests.
